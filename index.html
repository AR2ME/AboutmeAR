All about bubble tea!

Bubble tea which is commonly called boba, comes in milk and fruity varieties, and it can be served hot or cold. Tapioca, the tiny "pearls/bubbles" that are in this beverage, is the reason it is named bubble tea. It also referes to when the drink is shaken tiny bubbles are formed on top!

Did you know?

Bubble tea originated from Taiwan!

Toronto has over 100 bubble tea shops!

The OG boba just has black tea, milk and tapioca!
